## Sum formulas

$$(a + b)^2 = a^2 + 2ab + b^2$$

$$ \sum_{k=1}^{n} k = \frac{n(n + 1)}{2}  $$

$$ \sum_{k=1}^{n} k^2 = \frac{n(n + 1)(2n + 1)}{6}  $$

$$ \sum_{k=1}^{n} k^3 = \frac{n(n + 1)(2n + 1)}{6}  $$

$$ \sum_{k=1}^{n} k^4 = \frac{n(n+1)(2n + 1)(3n^2 + 3n - 1)}{30}$$
